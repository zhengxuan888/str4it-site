Shipping & Returns

Everything you need to know about delivery, returns, and exchanges

At Strait Pristine, we ship worldwide from our warehouse in Singapore. All orders are carefully packaged to ensure your items arrive in perfect condition. We use premium, eco-friendly packaging materials that reflect our commitment to sustainability.

Order Processing

All orders are processed within 1–2 business days (Monday to Friday, excluding public holidays). You will receive a confirmation email with tracking details once your order has been dispatched.

Customs & Import Duties

For international orders, please note that customs duties and import taxes may apply upon delivery. These charges are the responsibility of the recipient and are not included in the shipping cost. We recommend contacting your local customs office for more information before placing an order.

Packaging

Every Strait Pristine order is lovingly wrapped in our signature tissue paper and placed in a branded box made from recycled materials. Garments are folded with care and protected with acid-free paper. Orders are shipped in sturdy, water-resistant outer packaging to ensure safe delivery regardless of weather conditions.

Returns & Exchanges

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not, we offer a 30-day return and exchange policy on all full-price items.

Return Conditions

  • Items must be returned within 30 days of the delivery date
  • All items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original condition
  • Original tags must be attached
  • Original packaging must be included
  • Sale items and made-to-order items are final sale and cannot be returned
  • Undergarments, swimwear, and beauty products cannot be returned for hygiene reasons

How to Initiate a Return

  • Email us at service@str4it.com with your order number and reason for return
  • Our team will provide you with a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number and return address within 1–2 business days
  • Package your item securely and include the RMA number inside the parcel
  • Ship the package using a trackable shipping method — return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item is defective or incorrect

Exchange Policy

Exchanges are subject to product availability. If you'd like to exchange an item for a different size or colour, please initiate a return and place a new order for your desired item. We will process your refund once the returned item is received and inspected.

Refund Processing

Once we receive your returned item, please allow 5–7 business days for inspection and refund processing. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method. If you paid via PayPal, please allow an additional 3–5 business days for the refund to appear in your account.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery at service@str4it.com with photos of the issue. We will arrange for a replacement or full refund at no additional cost to you, including covering the return shipping.

Common Questions

How long does standard shipping take?

Within Singapore: 2-4 business days. International: 5-10 business days depending on destination.

Is express shipping available?

Yes, express shipping (2-3 business days within Singapore) is available for SGD 28. Select at checkout.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to over 15 countries. International customers may be subject to customs duties imposed by their country.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. Use this on the courier's website to follow your parcel.

What if my item arrives damaged?

Please email service@str4it.com with photos of the damaged item and your order number within 7 days of delivery. We'll arrange a replacement or refund.
