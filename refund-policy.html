Refund Policy

Our commitment to your satisfaction

Last updated: January 2025

Thank you for shopping with Strait Pristine. We are committed to providing you with the highest quality products and customer service. If you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help.

Refund Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund, the following conditions must be met:

  • The request must be made within 30 days of the date you received your item
  • The item must be unused, in the same condition that you received it, with all original tags attached
  • The item must be returned in its original packaging
  • You must provide a receipt or proof of purchase (order confirmation email)

Non-Refundable Items

The following items cannot be refunded:

  • Sale items — Items purchased at a discounted price are final sale
  • Gift cards — Non-refundable and non-exchangeable
  • Made-to-order / custom items — Items specifically made or customised for you
  • Intimate apparel — Undergarments, swimwear, and hosiery for hygiene reasons
  • Beauty & fragrance products — Once opened, for health and safety reasons
  • Items damaged through customer misuse — Damage caused by improper care, alterations, or accidents

Refund Process

Step 1: Contact us at service@str4it.com with your order number and reason for return.

Step 2: Our customer service team will review your request and provide a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number within 1–2 business days.

Step 3: Pack your item securely in its original packaging, include the RMA number, and ship to the address provided. We recommend using a trackable shipping service or purchasing shipping insurance.

Step 4: Once we receive and inspect your returned item (within 5–7 business days), we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

Step 5: If approved, your refund will be processed and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within 5–10 business days.

Refund Methods

Refunds are processed to the original payment method used at the time of purchase:

  • PayPal: Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days and will appear in your PayPal account
  • Credit/Debit Cards: Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days, though it may take an additional billing cycle for your bank to post the credit
  • Bank Transfer: Refunds are processed within 7–14 business days

Partial Refunds

In certain situations, only partial refunds may be granted:

  • Any item not in its original condition, is damaged, or missing parts for reasons not due to our error
  • Any item that is returned more than 30 days after delivery

Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received a refund yet, please:

  • Check your bank account again
  • Contact your credit card company — it may take some time before your refund is officially posted
  • Contact your bank — there is often some processing time before a refund is posted
  • If you've done all of this and still have not received your refund, please contact us at service@str4it.com

Defective or Incorrect Items

If you receive a defective, damaged, or incorrect item, please contact us immediately at service@str4it.com within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange for a replacement to be sent to you or provide a full refund including all shipping costs. Please include photos of the defect or incorrect item to help us resolve the issue as quickly as possible.
