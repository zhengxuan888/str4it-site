Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ordering, shipping, returns, and more.

Orders & Payment

We accept PayPal, Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Apple Pay. All transactions are processed securely through encrypted payment gateways. Your payment information is never stored on our servers.

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 2 hours of placement by emailing service@str4it.com with your order number. After this window, orders enter our fulfilment process and can no longer be modified. You may return items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this to track your parcel on the courier's website. Orders typically ship within 1-2 business days of placement.

Shipping & Delivery

Standard shipping is complimentary on orders over SGD 150. For orders under SGD 150, standard shipping is SGD 15 within Singapore and SGD 25 for international destinations. Express shipping (2-3 business days) is available for SGD 28.

Within Singapore: Standard delivery takes 2-4 business days, express delivery takes 1-2 business days. International orders: Standard delivery takes 5-10 business days depending on destination. We ship to over 15 countries including Australia, UK, US, Japan, and across Southeast Asia.

Yes! We ship to over 15 countries worldwide. International customers are responsible for any customs duties or import taxes imposed by their country. These fees are not included in our shipping charges. Please check your local customs regulations before ordering.

Returns & Exchanges

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn, unwashed items in their original condition with tags attached. Sale items are eligible for exchange or store credit only. To initiate a return, email service@str4it.com with your order number. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days of receiving the returned item.

Absolutely. We offer free size exchanges within Singapore. Simply email us at service@str4it.com within 30 days of delivery, and we'll arrange a courier pickup and send out the new size. International customers can return items for store credit and place a new order.

Each product page includes detailed size measurements and fit notes. We recommend measuring yourself and comparing with our size chart. Our garments generally fit true to size. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a relaxed fit or sizing down for a more tailored look. Contact us if you need assistance.

Products & Care

Yes. We work exclusively with family-run ateliers and artisan workshops across Southeast Asia. All our partners pay fair wages and provide safe working conditions. We conduct regular site visits and maintain long-term relationships with each production partner. Read more on our About page.

We recommend dry cleaning for all silk garments to preserve the fabric's lustre and hand-feel. If hand-washing, use cold water with a mild silk detergent, do not wring or twist, and lay flat to dry away from direct sunlight. Store on padded hangers. Iron on the lowest heat setting with a pressing cloth.
