Our Story

Our Origins

Born at the Crossroads of Civilisations

Strait Pristine was founded in 2022 in Singapore — a city that has stood for centuries at the meeting point of the world's great trading routes. The Straits of Malacca, through which goods, ideas, and artistry have flowed for millennia, is the wellspring of our creative vision.

Our founder drew inspiration from the Peranakan heritage of the region — a culture born from the marriage of Chinese and Malay traditions — and from the broader legacy of the maritime Silk Road.

Every piece we create carries within it this spirit of cultural exchange: the boldness of tropical colour, the refinement of Eastern craft, and the timeless appeal of considered design.

Our Journey

Key Milestones

2022

The Beginning

Founded in Singapore by Amara Chen. Launched our debut collection of 12 silk kaftans, selling out within 3 weeks through word-of-mouth alone.

2023

Expanding Horizons

2024

Going Global

Launched international shipping to 15 countries. Partnered with artisans in Java, Bali, and Pekalongan. Reached 10,000 happy customers worldwide.

2025

Sustainable Future

Achieved 100% sustainable packaging. Launched our Transparency Report. Introduced made-to-order programme to reduce waste. Growing our team to serve you better.

Our Commitment

Sustainability & Ethics

At Strait Pristine, sustainability isn't a marketing initiative — it's the foundation of how we do business. We believe that luxury fashion and environmental responsibility are not mutually exclusive.

We work exclusively with family-run ateliers across Southeast Asia, ensuring fair wages, safe working conditions, and the preservation of traditional craft techniques. Every artisan in our supply chain is paid above market rate.

Our packaging is 100% recyclable and plastic-free. We use GOTS-certified organic cotton, OEKO-TEX certified silks, and vegetable-tanned leathers. In 2024, we introduced a made-to-order programme to further reduce waste.

Our Craft

From Fibre to Fashion

The journey of a Strait Pristine piece begins with the careful selection of the finest natural materials — pure silks sourced from heritage mills, organic cottons from sustainable farms, and luxuriously soft linens.

Our print designs are developed in-house, drawing from the botanical richness of Southeast Asian gardens, the geometric patterns of Islamic art, and the flowing motifs of traditional batik.

Working with family-run ateliers across the region, we ensure that every stitch, every seam, and every finish meets our exacting standards of quality.

The Founder

A Vision Rooted in Heritage

Growing up between the bustling markets of Singapore and the ancient textile centres of Southeast Asia, our founder developed an early appreciation for the power of fabric to tell stories and connect people across cultures.

After years in international fashion and luxury goods, the desire to create something authentically rooted in the region's heritage — yet modern and globally appealing — led to the founding of Strait Pristine in 2022.

"I wanted to create a brand that celebrates the beauty of cultural exchange — the kind of beauty that has always existed at the crossroads of the world's great trading routes."

— Amara Chen, Founder & Creative Director
