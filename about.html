Our Story

From the crossroads of East and West, Strait Pristine brings you a world of color, craftsmanship, and contemporary elegance.

Where Tradition Meets Tomorrow

Strait Pristine was born from a deep love of artisanal craftsmanship and the belief that beautiful, hand-printed textiles should be part of everyday life. Based in Singapore, at the crossroads of Asia's rich textile traditions, we bring together the finest printing techniques from India, Indonesia, and Japan to create pieces that are both timeless and modern.

Our founder, inspired by the vibrant markets of Rajasthan, the batik workshops of Java, and the refined aesthetics of Japanese textiles, envisioned a brand that would celebrate the artistry of hand-printing while embracing contemporary design sensibility.

Our Craft

Every Strait Pristine piece begins with a hand-drawn design, translated into carved blocks or screens by master artisans. Using traditional techniques passed down through generations — from hand block printing to screen printing — each pattern is applied with care and precision.

We work exclusively with natural fibers: premium silk, breathable cotton, and luxurious silk-cotton blends. Our dyes are carefully selected for their vibrancy and longevity, ensuring that each piece maintains its beauty wash after wash.

Artisanal

Every piece is handcrafted using traditional printing techniques passed down through generations of master artisans.

Sustainable

We use natural fibers and eco-conscious processes, minimizing waste while maximizing beauty and quality.

Timeless

Our designs transcend trends, creating pieces that become treasured wardrobe staples for years to come.

Our Collections

From our iconic Nizam print — inspired by the geometric patterns of Mughal architecture — to the romantic florals of our Ginza Bouquet collection, each line tells a story of cultural convergence and artistic excellence.

Our fashion collections span dresses, kaftans, kurtas, and beachwear, while our home collection brings the same artistry to cushions, tableware, porcelain, and decorative accessories.

Singapore, Our Home

Headquartered at 8 Eu Tong Sen Street in the heart of Singapore, Strait Pristine is proud to be part of this vibrant city's rich textile heritage. Singapore's position as a cultural crossroads — where Chinese, Malay, Indian, and Western traditions meet — perfectly mirrors our design philosophy of bringing diverse artisanal traditions together in harmony.

We invite you to explore our world of color and craftsmanship, and to discover the pieces that will bring joy to your wardrobe and home.

— The Strait Pristine Team
