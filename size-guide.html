How to Measure

Bust

Measure around the fullest part of your bust, keeping the tape measure level across your back.

Waist

Measure around your natural waistline, which is the narrowest part of your torso.

Hips

Measure around the fullest part of your hips, keeping the tape measure level.

Length

Dress lengths are measured from the highest point of the shoulder to the hem.
